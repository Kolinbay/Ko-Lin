Introducing KDHW Pharmacy Data Management

Welcome to our innovative approach to data management at KDHW Pharmacy. In early 2023, we took a significant step towards modernizing our operations by adopting the Oracle Apex service.

Our data management system is designed to be user-friendly, making it accessible to individuals with intermediate-level computing knowledge. As long as you have internet access, you can benefit from this system's capabilities.

One of the outstanding features of our system is its affordability. It's an ideal solution for community-based organizations and non-governmental organizations, as it minimizes costs while maximizing efficiency.
