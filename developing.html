KDHW Pharmacy Data Management Software Developing

 What platform we use for our data software?

Our KDHW Pharmacy uses the Oracle Apex platform to operate our pharmacy inventory software. It's a user-friendly, low-code platform that's fast and efficient, saving developers time. As it is low-code, the developer feel more confortable using it to develop the software, making database process quick and effecient for his/her need base on the demand of the organization.

 What Special Skill need for developer?

The developers need intermediate IT skills and proficiency in English to build software, fix bugs, as the platform is primarily in English.

 What do we need to do regularly with our database process?

Based on Oracle's updates, we must ensure the necessary data flow and a great user experience to make people more tech-savvy.
