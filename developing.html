KDHW Pharmacy Data Management Software Developing

 What platform we use for our data software?

Our KDHW Pharmacy uses the Oracle Apex platform to operate our pharmacy inventory software. It's a user-friendly, low-code platform that's fast and efficient, saving developers time. As it is low-code, the developer feel more confortable using it to develop the software, making database process quick and effecient for his/her need base on the demand of the organization.

 What Special Skill need for developer?

The developers need intermediate IT skills and proficiency in English to build software, fix bugs, as the platform is primarily in English.

 What do we need to do regularly with our database process?

Based on Oracle's updates, we must ensure the necessary data flow and a great user experience to make people more tech-savvy.

Oracle Apex

 Data Entry

When we offer training and equip our field staff with the necessary skills, our central team also plays a crucial role in maintaining consistency in our data entry processes. This becomes especially important when we deliver items to the field and need to perform inventory balancing. In such situations, our central team follows the same data entry procedures as the field staff. This uniform approach ensures that our inventory management remains accurate and up-to-date, guaranteeing the smooth flow of operations and the availability of essential pharmaceutical products in the field. By maintaining this consistency, we ensure that our inventory management is efficient and dependable, which is vital to achieving our organizational goals and delivering quality service to our customers.

Oracle Database

  Data Analyze Assistance

Data analysis is a vital component of our data maintenance and management processes. It plays a crucial role in ensuring that the data we've entered is not only accurate but also useful for decision-making. To achieve this, it's essential for our team to understand how to analyze the data effectively once the data entry process is complete. The focal person, who is a key point of contact, assists our field data staff in keeping the entered data up to date and correct. We empower our field staff with essential data analysis skills, including the use of basic tools like Microsoft Excel and formatting within it. We also stress the importance of quick assistance whenever they require it, to keep them motivated and ensure they are well-prepared for their future tasks in data management. By sharing these fundamental data analysis skills and offering support, we aim to maintain a high standard of data quality and foster a sense of confidence among our field staff as they contribute to our data management goals.
