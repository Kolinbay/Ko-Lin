Our Pharmacy Team

What our team do?

Our team is a large and friendly group situated along the Myanmar-Thai border and within the Karen State. We collaborate closely as part of a vast network, all working towards a shared goal of achieving success to Kaw Thoo Lei Health System. In this unique position, we serve as the intermediary between the providers and recipients, effectively bridging the gap to connect donors' management with the clients' needs. We operate in harmony, extending help to one another when the need arises and offering motivation and support during times of weakness. While our team may not receive high wages, we find fulfillment in our work, driven by the desire to create a stronger, more vibrant community for our people. This shared purpose unites us, and our commitment to the cause fuels our dedication to making a positive impact in the region toward KawThooLei.



Our Organization

What our organization do?

Our organization is dedicated to providing healthcare services across different locations in the Karen State, with the primary goal of enhancing the health and well-being of every individual in the community. We understand the financial challenges faced by the population, and that's why our services are offered free of charge. As a community-based organization, we rely on partnerships and collaboration with various stakeholders to ensure the sustainability and effectiveness of our healthcare initiatives. Our ultimate mission is to serve as a guiding motto for "health for all," striving to make healthcare accessible and inclusive, irrespective of one's financial circumstances. Through our commitment to this noble cause, we aim to create a healthier and more equitable environment for the people we serve.
