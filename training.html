Training & Coordination
Training

Our central team offers training programs aimed at improving knowledge about using pharmacy management software effectively. The training focuses on understanding how to utilize the software efficiently and how to maintain and analyze data, ultimately enhancing the overall efficiency of our pharmacy management system to meet our goals. We provide two types of training: in-person sessions and online courses. These training opportunities are available for our field data staff, ensuring that they have the necessary skills to handle our data management processes effectively. Additionally, we follow up with our staff regularly after the training to provide ongoing support and guidance, ensuring that our pharmacy management remains optimized and effective.

Coordination

Our Central also provide the coordination meeting to our field staff to manage their pharmacy data nicely and to improve the correction of the data management. We know that without coordination, the field staff will have some gaps during data management and it will some how negatively effect our pharmacy data and recording quality.

The coordination usually focus on the main person of field level staff in order to manage their staff as related and regular monitoring the senior management staff toward the central level. We see the result of coordination as a good stratergy to improve our task toward our organization goal.
